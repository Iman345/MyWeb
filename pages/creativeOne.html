Data Vizualization Three: Vertical Bar graph 2

Total Deaths per province and total active cases on November 1st

Bar graph with changing data sets

^

Bar graph Two Analysis

This is a bar chart that depicts two data sets by clicking the buttons. The first data set shows the deaths per province and the second shows the amount of active cases per province as of November 1st. This data can be seen in a pie chart in a previous data visualization. However, the bar chart effectively communicates the data where the pie chart does not. Here we can noticeably see the trends and comprehend where COVID 19 has the largest infection rate. The pie chart made it confusing and difficult to comprehend this information because it was hard to compare the slices.
